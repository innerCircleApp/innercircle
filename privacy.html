Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data such as name, age, profile details, and photos.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide matching, messaging, and profile features.
  • To protect member safety and prevent abuse.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and support quality.

3. Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers required to operate the app (for example, hosting, authentication, and messaging infrastructure) and where legally required.

4. Data Retention

We retain data while your account is active and for limited periods after closure when needed for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute handling.

5. Your Choices

  • Update profile details directly in the app.
  • Request account deletion through in-app profile settings.
  • Manage notification permissions in iOS settings.

6. Security

We use technical and organizational measures designed to protect user data, including access controls and service-level safeguards.
