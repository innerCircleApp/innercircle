Common Questions

How do matches work?
When two members both tap "Interested," a mutual match is created and chat unlocks.

How can I report or block someone?
Use in-app profile options to report behavior or block a member immediately.

How do I delete my account?
Go to your profile settings in the app and choose Delete Account.
